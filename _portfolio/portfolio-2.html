--- title: "VocabuStory" excerpt: "An LLM-powered Flutter app for efficient language learning through narrative generation." collection: portfolio ---

VocabuStory is an LLM-powered Flutter application that improves language learning efficiency through customized narrative generation. Rather than rote memorization, VocabuStory embeds target vocabulary into engaging, personalized stories generated on-demand by a large language model, making acquisition more natural and effective.

Technologies: Flutter / Dart, LLM APIs
