挪啦 is a Flutter application designed to detect critical SMS messages and trigger real-time alerts. The app intelligently parses incoming messages, identifies high-priority content (such as verification codes, financial alerts, or emergency notifications), and surfaces them to the user immediately.

**Status:** Deployed on China's major Android app galleries and market-ready.

**Technologies:** Flutter / Dart, Android
