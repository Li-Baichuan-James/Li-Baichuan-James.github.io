Hi! I'm Baichuan, a Year 2 undergraduate student in Computer Engineering at the [Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)](https://hkust.edu.hk/). My current CGA is 4.141/4.3, placing me in the top 1% of my cohort.

My research interests lie in **large language models (LLMs)**, **multi-modal AI**, and **mobile application development**. I enjoy building systems that bridge intelligent models and real-world user experiences.

Research
======
I am currently involved in two research projects:

- **AutoTour** – An automatic photo tour guide system that uses smartphones and LLMs to provide context-aware travel narratives. [[arXiv]](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.06781)
- **MMRouter** – A routing mechanism for multi-modal input queries that achieves an optimal cost-performance tradeoff in model selection.

Projects
======
- **挪啦 (NuoLa)** – A Flutter app for critical SMS detection and real-time alert triggering. Deployed on China's major Android app stores.
- **VocabuStory** – An LLM-powered Flutter app that improves language learning efficiency through customized narrative generation.
