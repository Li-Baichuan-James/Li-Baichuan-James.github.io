AutoTour is an automatic photo tour guide system that leverages smartphones and large language models (LLMs) to deliver context-aware travel narratives. By combining on-device photo capture with LLM-powered scene understanding, AutoTour enables rich, personalized touring experiences without requiring manual input from the user.
